The Barbershop

Being a licensed barber for over five years, my dream was to always open up a barbershop that specialized in traditional hair cuts. I wanted to bring the old school way of barbering to the new school way of doing things. Razor & Whiskey Barbershop is the perfect blend of the two. You can enjoy a complimentary glass of whiskey while you receive a traditional service that includes hot towel shaves and beard trims. After your cut, lounge out and relax while catching up on sports, or sit at our bistro table and enjoy a board game or two. Razor & Whiskey is all about the experience.

Wedding Business

Starting the business in 2018, I wanted to create something that catered to the groom and groomsmen on their big day. This new experience, I'll bring the barbershop to you, while you enjoy complimentary whiskey and cigars. Razor & Whiskey is meant to make you feel good and confident on one of the biggest days of your life. The men simply forget that its both the bride & groom getting married, not just the bride.. Razor & Whiskey is something every man can enjoy and will never forget.
